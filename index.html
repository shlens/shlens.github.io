Jon Shlens

I am a senior staff scientist at Google Brain. I am broadly interested in the topics of vision, language and learning including:

  • Development of new methods of image processing, computer vision and machine learning.
  • Building an understanding how vision works in artificial and biological systems.
I am a co-inventor of TensorFlow, developing many of its widely deployed vision systems. To learn more about my work, please explore my publications or tutorials.
